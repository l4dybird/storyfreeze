# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in StoryFreeze a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity,
experience level, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate with empathy and respect.
- Give and accept constructive technical feedback.
- Focus criticism on ideas and changes, not people.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's private information.
- Help keep project spaces productive and welcoming.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualized conduct,
trolling, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact
method listed on their GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and
handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may edit or remove content,
reject contributions, issue warnings, or temporarily or permanently exclude
participants when necessary to protect the community.

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, project
events, and situations where someone is representing StoryFreeze.
